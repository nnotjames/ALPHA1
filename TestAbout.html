What is TechConnect?

A free ICT Training Program

TechConnect: Palangoy

A community program that gives out-of-school youth in Barangay Palangoy the chance to learn computer skills that can open doors to better opportunities. Through friendly, hands-on training, participants how to use a computer, navigate the internet, communicate online, and work with basic software skills that can help them apply for jobs, start small businesses, or continue their studies. The heart of the program is simple: to help young people feel confident in using technology so they can take steps toward a brighter future and be part of a community that’s ready for the digital world.

Functionality

•Digital Skills Training – Hands-on lessons in basic computer operations, internet use, online communication, and productivity tools.

•Job Readiness Support – Equips participants with the technological skills needed to apply for jobs or start small online businesses.

•Accessible Community Program – Conducted within Barangay Palangoy, removing barriers like travel costs and long commutes.

•Practical Learning Approach – Real-life tasks and exercises so learners can apply their skills immediately.

•Inclusive and Supportive Environment – Encourages learning at one’s own pace in a friendly, non-intimidating setting.

Benefits

•Increased Employment Opportunities – Opens doors to jobs requiring basic computer literacy.

•Empowerment Through Technology – Builds confidence to engage in the digital economy.

•Pathway to Further Education – Serves as a foundation for advanced ICT or vocational training.

•Economic Uplift – Enables participants to explore freelancing, online selling, or other income streams.

•Stronger Community Engagement – Encourages active participation in local initiatives through digital platforms.

•Bridging the Digital Divide – Ensures no youth in the community is left behind in the modern technological era.
